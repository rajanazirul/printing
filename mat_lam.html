2 UP Size > 54mm x 90mm and Size <108mm x 90mm 2 x Single Business card Price
2 UP Size > 54mm x 90mm and Size <54mm x 180mm 2 x Single Business card Price
4 UP Size > 108mm x 90mm and Size <108mm x 180mm 4 x Single Business card Price
6 UP Size > 108mm x 180mm and Size <108mm x 270mm 6 x Single Business card Price
6 UP Size > 108mm x 180mm and Size <162mm x 180mm 6 x Single Business card Price
8 UP Size > 108mm x 270mm and Size <108mm x 360mm 8 x Single Business card Price
8 UP Size > 162mm x 180mm and Size <216mm x 180mm 8 x Single Business card Price
9 UP Size > 108mm x 180mm and Size <162mm x 270mm 9 x Single Business card Price
12 UP Size > 162mm x 270mm and Size <162mm x 360mm 12 x Single Business card Price
12 UP Size > 162mm x 270mm and Size <216mm x 270mm 12 x Single Business card Price
16 UP Size > 162mm x 270mm and Size <216mm x 360mm 16 x Single Business card Price
**You can order as many up as you wish. The number of UP will calculate based on square or rectangle shape.
